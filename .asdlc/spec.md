# Overview

An internal application where WSO2 employees request time off and managers approve or deny those requests.

# Personas

- Employee — requests leave and checks their balance
- Manager — reviews and responds to leave requests from their team
- HR administrator — manages leave policies and employee allowances

# Features

- An employee submits a leave request for specific dates with a reason
- Managers see pending requests from their direct reports and approve or reject them
- Employees view their remaining leave balance by type (vacation, sick, personal)
- HR sets annual leave allowances for each employee
- Everyone sees their upcoming approved leave on a calendar view
